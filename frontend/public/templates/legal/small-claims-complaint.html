WHAT HAPPENS NEXT:

  1. File this form with your local small claims court clerk.
  2. Pay the filing fee (or submit a fee waiver request if you cannot afford to pay).
  3. The court will provide you with a court date and instructions on how to serve the defendant.
  4. You must properly serve the defendant with a copy of this complaint and the court summons according to your state's rules.
  5. Appear at the scheduled court hearing prepared to present your case and any evidence.
